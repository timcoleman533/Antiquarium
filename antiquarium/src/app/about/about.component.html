The Antiquarium Ltd...

...is a retail outlet containing many independant traders selling antiques, vintage, handmade goods, curios, jewellery and artwork. We are open 7 days a week and can be located in the beautiful Ordnance Depot in Weedon Bec.

Looking for antiques?

We might have what you're looking for!

Where to find us:

From Towcester:
Travel North up the A5 until you see a turning to the left for Bridge Street. Follow it until you can turn right onto Harman's Way. Then take your first left through the gate to the Munitions Depot. The Antiquarium is the left-hand side of the first building on the right.

From Rugby:
Travel South down the A5 until you see a turning to the right for Bridge Street. Follow it until you can turn right onto Harman's Way. Then take your first left through the gate to the Munitions Depot. The Antiquarium is the left-hand side of the first building on the right.

From Daventry:
Travel East along the A45 until you reach a roundabout that allows you onto High Street. From there look for a turning on the right to Harman's Way. Follow Harman's Way until you reach a turning on your right to the Munitions Depot. The Antiquarium is the left-hand side of the first building on the right.

From Weedon Bec:
Travel North up Bridge Street and over the River Nene. Keep following until you reach Harman's Way. Follow Harman's Way and take the first left into the Munitions Depot. The Antiquarium is the left-hand side of the first building on the right.
